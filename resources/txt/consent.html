Privacy

If you synchronize your tournament with the online server, all data concerning players and matches will be made publicly available on the internet. This pertains in particular to player names, matches, match results and match times. By registering your tournament with the server, you confirm that all participants of your tournament have been informed about and have consented to the publication of this data.

All data transfers related to the online service will be unencrypted. This includes personal information such as your name or your email address as well as all tournament information (player names, match results, ...).


Responsible usage

You are responsible for the data that is published to the server. Refrain from pushing inappropriate or illegal content to the server. For instance, do not abuse free text fields like the tournament name or player names for inappropriate purposes.


Beta service

The online synchronization service of QTournament is still in a Beta phase. It has been tested and is likely to work, but there is no guarantee whatsoever that the service will be available and/or working correctly. Bug reports are welcome but don't blame me if things didn't work for you... ;)
